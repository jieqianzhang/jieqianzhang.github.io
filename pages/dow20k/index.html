How the Dow Breached 20000

Five stocks gave the Dow Jones Industrial Average a huge boost on its way from its March 9, 2009, low to its first close above 20000. Heavyweights include Boeing, which contributed 993.8 points to the Dow during this time. At the other extreme, Apple—the latest entrant to the Dow, replacing AT&T in 2015—subtracted 41.8 points.
