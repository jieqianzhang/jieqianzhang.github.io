How Much Do CEOs Make?

We analyzed S&P 500 companies' proxies data and visualized how much CEOs make. Median pay reached $12.1 million for CEOs of the biggest U.S. companies in 2017, a new post-recession high, as profits and stock prices soared. Most S&P 500 CEOs received raises of 9.7% or better last year.
